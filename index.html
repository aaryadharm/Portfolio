Analyzing Google Translate's Success on a Low Resource Language

January 3, 2022 - March 11, 2022

Translation is highly complicated and subjective process that people have been trying to replicate with machines for decades. I tested both the translation and speech-to-text feature of Google Translate in a low resource language, Marathi, in a consumer type use. I analyzed both features along with the user experience.

Foster care and child welfare is a topic I am very passionate about and want to improve the system using data science. While taking classes at Stanford, I decided to take the first step and analyze some of the public data to give insights on where more funding should go.
